Problem Statement

The primary objective of our documentation embraces the development of an advanced target-locking system to create a sophisticated system capable of detecting, tracking, and predicting the movement of a dynamic target in real-time. The model must be solidly built, have minimal latency, and offer high accuracy in locking onto a target in varying conditions like: Predict the target's trajectory, Real-time adjustments, Adapting to the motion.
